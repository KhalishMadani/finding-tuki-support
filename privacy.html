Privacy Policy

The Short Version

Finding Tuki does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. There are no ads, no analytics trackers, and no third-party SDKs watching what you do. Your game data stays on your device, and online leaderboards run entirely through Apple's Game Center.

What the Game Stores on Your Device

Finding Tuki saves the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your scores and run history (score, energy, timestamps)
  • Your best score
  • Your settings — music and sound-effect volume, haptics on/off, language (English / Indonesian)
  • Whether you have completed the tutorial

This data never leaves your device, is not linked to your identity by us, and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Game Center (Leaderboards)

The online leaderboard uses Apple Game Center, a service provided by Apple. If you are signed in to Game Center, your scores are submitted to Apple along with your Game Center player identity so they can appear on the leaderboard under your Game Center nickname.

We do not operate our own servers and never receive this data ourselves. Game Center is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. You can play the game without signing in to Game Center — the leaderboard simply won't be available.

What We Don't Do

  • No accounts or sign-up — we never ask for your name, email, or password
  • No advertising or ad networks
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No selling or sharing of data with third parties
  • No access to your contacts, photos, location, camera, or microphone

Children's Privacy

Finding Tuki is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data, no personal information is knowingly collected from children. Leaderboard participation via Game Center is managed through the device's Apple Account and its parental-control settings (Screen Time / Families).

Changes to This Policy

If the game's data practices ever change (for example, if a future update adds an online feature), this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date revised before the change takes effect.
